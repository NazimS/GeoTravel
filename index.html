About Us

Geo Travel – is stable, progressively developing company. Our team consists of qualified advisers and managers having considerable experience of work. Company Geo Travel is founded by professionals, people who have devoted their life to tourism. Having a wide experience, Geo Travel manages tours in 45 countries of the world, realizes exclusive programs: training and medical treatment abroad, exclusive participation in cultural and entertainment actions, organization of business trips and business seminars for experts of various branches.

DISCOVER A DESTINATION WITH A STORY TO TELL FROM INTRIGUING CITIES TO HIGH PASSES

At will of the customer Geo Travel can reserve any of thousand of the best hotels of the world, charter the yacht or private plane, rent prestigious limousine, comfortable country house, or ancient castle. Geo Travel is a member of JATA (the Japanese Association of Travel Agents) and IATA (International Air Transport Asotiation). For FIT tourists: Specializing on organization of individual tours, we offer the travelers new possibility – not to bind themselves with frameworks of a group trip, but to create and plan your own rest with comfortable conditions suitable for them. Intimate knowledge of features of each country, received by managers during training and seminars, guarantees clients of Geo Travel high level of service.
